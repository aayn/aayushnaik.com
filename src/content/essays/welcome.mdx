Welcome, internet stranger. I don't link out to these personal essays anywhere on the internet, so either you were specifically looking for this or I've become famous enough for a search index search engine to index this obscure link to which nothing points. Either way, I'm glad you found it. I think of this place as my own place on the internet, my internet home, and as such, most things here are raw, unpolished, personal, and sometimes rather dark.

And in case you're wondering, yes, a lot of the epistemological tags and sidenotes style is inspired by Gwern. 

Each of the essays either reflects on my opinion about a certain topic out there in the world or dives deeper into my internal thought process. 

My opinions should not be mere statements, but they should go along with how certain I am of them and to what extent I agree with them or not, or how strongly I hold them. This meta-epistemic commentary helps to encode all of that and allows me to state my opinions more clearly and honestly.
